## 🇬🇧 About

CeDak is a productivity and study-tracking application built to help users improve focus, build consistency, and visualize their progress.

Rather than being just another timer application, CeDak provides tools that encourage discipline, goal achievement, and long-term habit formation.

It is designed for students, self-learners, and professionals who want to stay productive and organized.

---

# ✨ Features

### ⏱️ Focus Sessions
Start study or work sessions with a single tap and stay focused on your goals.

### 📈 Progress Tracking
Track daily, weekly, and long-term performance through detailed statistics.

### 🎯 Goal Management
Set personal goals and monitor your completion rates.

### 🔥 Streak System
Build consistency through streak tracking and maintain motivation.

### 📊 Analytics & Insights
Visualize your productivity with clean and understandable charts.

### 📚 Category-Based Tracking
Analyze your performance across different study or work categories.

### 🔔 Smart Notifications
Stay on track with reminders and motivational notifications.

### 🎨 Clean User Experience
Modern, minimal, and distraction-free interface designed for productivity.

# 🚀 Why CeDak?

Most productivity applications focus on tracking time.

CeDak focuses on building consistency.

The goal is not simply to measure how long users work, but to help them develop sustainable habits, stay motivated, and continuously improve.
